Privacy Policy

CRP Focus & related services – Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CRP collects, uses, and protects information across CRP Focus (mobile app), early access website, and CRP Editor platform.

By using any CRP service, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

CRP Focus Mobile App

We collect the following information and store it on our servers (Firebase):

  • Email address – for account creation and login.
  • Name – a display name derived from your email (stored with your account).
  • User IDs – Firebase User ID and device identifiers used for pairing.
  • Device information – device type, online status, and pairing data.
  • Crash reports and usage data – to improve stability and performance.

Notification content (titles, text, package names) is never uploaded to our servers. It stays on your device and is processed locally by the on‑device AI model to calculate importance scores. We do not have access to your personal messages.

On‑device AI Model

CRP Focus downloads a language model (approx. 2.5 GB) from a public repository (Hugging Face) to your device. This download is a one‑time file transfer; no personal data is sent to Hugging Face or any third party during or after the download.

Early Access Website & CRP Editor

We collect only your email address when you sign up for early access or create an editor account. No additional personal data is collected.

Landing Page

No personal data is collected.

3. How We Use Data

We use the collected data exclusively for:

  • Account creation and authentication (via Firebase).
  • Device pairing and synchronisation across your devices.
  • Improving app stability and performance (crash reports, analytics).
  • Enabling the on‑device AI classification (no data leaves your device for this).

4. Third‑Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) as our service provider for:

  • Authentication
  • Cloud database (Firestore, Realtime Database)
  • Crash reporting and analytics

Firebase processes data only on our behalf, as a data processor. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any independent third party.

The AI model file is downloaded from Hugging Face’s servers, but no personal data is transmitted during that download. The download is a standard file transfer.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third party for marketing, advertising, or any other independent purpose. Firebase acts only as a service provider under our control.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account data (email, name, user IDs, device pairings) for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your data: Visit https://crp-company.github.io/CRP-Account-Deletion/ and submit a deletion request. We will process it within 30 days.

Notification data stored locally on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from system settings.

7. Security

All data transmitted between your device and Firebase is encrypted in transit (TLS). Access to our backend is restricted to authorised personnel only. However, no system is 100% secure, and you use our services at your own risk.

8. Children’s Privacy

CRP services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via the app or website. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
